Bio

Jun 22nd, 2012

Matthew Gillingham is a software engineer living in Tokyo, Japan. For the past several years, he has primarily been involved in Objective-C, Javascript, Ruby and Python, working for companies such as Tonchidot and GREE. His projects have spanned from games to social networking platforms. He also leads the Tokyo iOS Developers Meetup, the largest English-language meetup group for iOS Developer’s in Tokyo area.

In addition to software development, he has a strong interest in social science, including a Master’s Degree in Global Studies at Sophia University.
